## 2. Description of Service

### 2.1 What Axon Does
Axon is a personal AI orchestration system that:
- Connects to third-party AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, xAI, and others)
- Stores conversations and memories locally on Your device
- Enables switching between AI models within conversations
- Provides memory-based context enhancement for AI interactions

### 2.2 What Axon Does NOT Do
- Axon does not provide AI capabilities itself—it connects to third-party AI services
- Axon does not store Your data on our servers (all data is stored locally on Your device or via Your iCloud account)
- Axon does not have access to Your API keys after You enter them (they are stored in Your device's secure Keychain)

## 4. Data and Privacy

### 4.1 Local-First Architecture
Axon is designed with privacy as a core principle:
- **Conversations** are stored locally on Your device using SwiftData
- **Memories** are stored locally on Your device
- **API keys** are stored in Your device's secure Keychain
- **No data is transmitted to methodolojee servers**

### 4.2 iCloud Sync (Optional)
If You enable iCloud sync:
- Your data syncs through Your personal iCloud account
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest per Apple's iCloud security practices
- We do not have access to Your iCloud data
- You can disable sync at any time in Settings

### 4.3 Third-Party Data Transmission
When You use AI features, Your messages are transmitted to:
- The AI provider You have selected (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.)
- Subject to that provider's privacy policy and data practices

We encourage You to review the privacy policies of any AI providers You use.

### 4.4 Analytics
Axon may collect anonymized, aggregated usage analytics (such as feature usage frequency) to improve the App. This data:
- Contains no personal information
- Contains no conversation content
- Contains no API keys
- Can be disabled in Settings
